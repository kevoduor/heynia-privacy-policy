HeyNia Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 12, 2025

HeyNia (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you visit heynia.com or use our services.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Directly from you: name, email, phone, billing info, account credentials, support communications
  • Automatically: device info, IP address, browser type, cookies, usage data
  • From partners: payment processors, marketing platforms, analytics providers

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To process payments and fulfill orders
  • To communicate important updates and support
  • For marketing and personalized advertising (with opt-out options)
  • To protect against fraud and secure our platform

3. Sharing Your Information

We share your data only with trusted service providers who help run HeyNia, such as payment gateways, cloud hosts, and marketing partners. We never sell your personal info.

4. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict your data. You can also opt out of marketing communications at any time.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect usage info. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser.

6. Children’s Privacy

HeyNia does not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to delete it.

7. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect your data but cannot guarantee perfect security. We retain your data as needed to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We’ll notify you by updating the date and posting changes on our site.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, email us at hey@heynia.com.
